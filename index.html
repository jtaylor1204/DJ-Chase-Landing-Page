About DJ Chase

DJ Chase is the founder of New York City-based Worldwide Soundz Records and DJ Chase Publishing, LLC. As a professional DJ, he specializes in Hip Hop, R&B, Reggae/ Dance-Hall, old-school, and House music, performing in shows throughout Connecticut, Atlanta, Boston, and Lawrence MA. Mccoy's biggest hit to date, "Big Mad," was played by DJ Drewski on Hot 97 FM, before reaching 12,000 streams on Spotify as of 2020. DJ Chase has ascended into beat-making, dropping his most recent lo-fi effort, Monday Night Lights. His music catalog has garnered over 11 million streams independently.
